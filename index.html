Game Development

Since the age of 15 Xander Grice has had a passion for game development. It began in his year 9 high school IT teacher. He started building 3D maze games from the beginning, writing a maze generation algorithm at 15. He continued to look into terrain generation and wrote his first itearation of infinite terrain generation at 16. From there his interest in game development has only risen. At 21 he wrote his first gaming engine, since when he has only improved upon it. Now he is regarded as one of the best Indie game developers out there.
